What are the characteristics of our products?

Any configuration

Our products are produced in different sizes, versions, configurations. You can specify what you need in your office. Due to the fact that it is not always obvious and easy, we will gladly advise you and help you with the right options.

Any print

Each binder, folder or clipboard can have any print according to your design, not just outside, but also inside. We use modern digital or offset printing.

Glamorous finishing

This is what distinguishes your products, skilful selection of finishing elements, such as the type of a foil or UV varnish, guarantees the unique and special, durable character of the item being decorated.
